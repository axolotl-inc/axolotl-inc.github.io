Help

Controls:
If you chose WASD, W is forward, S is backword, A is left, and D is right.
If you chose Arrow Keys, ↑ is forward, ↓ is backward, ← is left, and → is right.
If you chose Mobile, well, thats broken...

Goal of the game:
The goal of Bristle is to spike other objects (and players) with your bristle! As you do this you will collect points. Every 40 points you get, you level up. Then you can choose a new bristle to use. This goes up to 4 levels
